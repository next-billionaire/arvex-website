Years of Craft

Maison Vëil was founded in 2010 by Isabelle Vëil in the ateliers of the 8th arrondissement. Every garment begins as a sketch on paper — hand-drawn, debated, and revised before a single thread is cut. We believe in slowness. In precision. In the radical act of making something that lasts.
