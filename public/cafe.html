Our Philosophy

A Café Born from
Obsession

Noir & Bloom began as a pursuit — to find the perfect cup and share it. We source directly from family-owned farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Japan, roasting in-house each morning to ensure every sip arrives at its peak expression.

Our space is designed for slowness. For conversation. For the quiet luxury of a moment truly savoured.
