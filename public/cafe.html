Where Design Meets Taste

Aura Brew is a premium speciality cafe offering globally sourced beans, signature desserts, artisan sandwiches, and curated ambience. Located in the heart of the city, we serve creators, professionals, and coffee lovers seeking more than caffeine.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to build a social living room, Aura Brew combines design psychology, sensory coffee experiences, and hospitality innovation. The cafe hosts events, book clubs, creator meetups, and live acoustic nights.
